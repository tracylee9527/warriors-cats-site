About Warriors

Warriors is a beloved fantasy novel series written under the pen name Erin Hunter (a collaborative team of authors). The series follows the lives of wild cats divided into four Clans — ThunderClan, ShadowClan, WindClan, and RiverClan — who live by an ancient warrior code in a vast forest. With over 80 books across multiple arcs, Warriors has captivated millions of readers worldwide since the first book, Into the Wild, was published in 2003.

Core Lore

⭐

StarClan

The warrior ancestors who watch over the living Clans from a starlit sky. They communicate through dreams and omens, guiding medicine cats and leaders.

🌑

The Dark Forest

Also called the Place of No Stars — where wicked cats go after death. An evil realm where banished warriors plot against the living Clans.

📜

The Warrior Code

A set of ancient rules all four Clans abide by — from defending Clan territory to respecting elders. The code is the backbone of Clan society.

🌿

Medicine Cats

Special cats trained in herbal healing and the interpretation of StarClan's signs. They serve as healers and spiritual guides for their Clan.
