Characters

The cats who shaped the destiny of the Clans.

⚠ Unofficial Fan Site — Not affiliated with Working Partners Ltd. or HarperCollins

The Warriors series features hundreds of memorable characters across its many books. Below are profiles of key cats — organized by Clan — with factual details drawn from across the series. All information is encyclopedic in nature (names, ranks, family ties, notable roles).

🔥 ThunderClan
🦊

Firestar StarClan

Leader of ThunderClan
  • Born name Rusty (kittypet)
  • Clan ThunderClan
  • Pelt Bright ginger tabby, green eyes
  • Mentor Bluestar
  • Mate Sandstorm
  • Kits Squirrelflight, Leafpool
  • Rank Warrior → Deputy → Leader

A former house cat (kittypet) who joined ThunderClan as an apprentice named Firepaw. He rose through the ranks on merit and eventually became one of the most celebrated leaders in Clan history. He is the protagonist of the original Warriors arc.

🐈‍⬛

Bluestar StarClan

Leader of ThunderClan
  • Born name Bluepaw / Bluefur
  • Clan ThunderClan
  • Pelt Blue-grey, ice-blue eyes
  • Sister Snowfur
  • Kits Mistyfoot, Stonefur, Mosskit
  • Rank Warrior → Deputy → Leader

A noble and deeply complex leader who sacrificed her kits to stop a dangerous rival from becoming deputy. She mentored Fireheart and saw greatness in the young kittypet. Her backstory is told in the super edition Bluestar's Prophecy.

🐱

Bramblestar Living

Leader of ThunderClan
  • Father Tigerstar
  • Mother Goldenflower
  • Clan ThunderClan
  • Pelt Dark brown tabby, amber eyes
  • Mate Squirrelflight
  • Rank Apprentice → Warrior → Deputy → Leader

Son of the notorious Tigerstar, Bramblestar chose loyalty and honor over his father's dark legacy. He became a trusted deputy under Firestar and eventually leader of ThunderClan. His storyline explores the struggle against inherited expectations.

🐈

Squirrelflight Living

Deputy of ThunderClan
  • Father Firestar
  • Mother Sandstorm
  • Clan ThunderClan
  • Pelt Dark ginger, green eyes
  • Sister Leafpool
  • Mate Bramblestar

Firestar's spirited and bold daughter. She journeyed with the questing cats in The New Prophecy arc and became deputy of ThunderClan. Known for her fierce independence and deep loyalty to those she loves.

🌑 ShadowClan
🐆

Tigerstar Dark Forest

Leader of ShadowClan / TigerClan
  • Born name Tigerclaw
  • Clan ThunderClan → ShadowClan
  • Pelt Dark brown tabby, amber eyes
  • Kits Bramblestar, Tawnypelt (w/ Goldenflower); Hawkfrost, Mothwing (w/ Sasha)
  • Rank Warrior → Exiled → ShadowClan Leader

Once ThunderClan's deputy, Tigerstar was exiled for treachery and ambition. He seized control of ShadowClan and attempted to unite the forest under his tyrannical rule. He is the primary villain of the original arc and one of the most iconic antagonists in the series.

🐈‍⬛

Tawnypelt Living

Senior Warrior of ShadowClan
  • Father Tigerstar
  • Mother Goldenflower
  • Clan ThunderClan → ShadowClan
  • Pelt Tortoiseshell, green eyes
  • Brother Bramblestar

Tigerstar's daughter who chose ShadowClan to forge her own identity, free from her father's shadow. She is known for her fierce independence and proving her loyalty to ShadowClan above all else. One of the chosen four who received the new prophecy.

🐱

Yellowfang StarClan

Medicine Cat
  • Clan ShadowClan → ThunderClan
  • Pelt Dark grey, scarred face, orange eyes
  • Former role ShadowClan Medicine Cat
  • Kit Brokenstar (secret)
  • Specialty Healing, wisdom, sharp wit

A gruff but compassionate medicine cat who left ShadowClan under Brokenstar's cruelty. She joined ThunderClan and mentored Cinderpelt. Her tragic backstory, including her secret son Brokenstar, is explored in the super edition Yellowfang's Secret.

💨 WindClan
🐇

Tallstar StarClan

Leader of WindClan
  • Clan WindClan
  • Pelt Black and white, dark amber eyes
  • Rank Warrior → Leader
  • Notable Longest-reigning WindClan leader
  • Ally Close friends with Firestar

One of the most respected leaders in Clan history, Tallstar ruled WindClan for many seasons and was known for his fairness and wisdom. His complex personal history is explored in Tallstar's Revenge. He appointed Onewhisker his successor with his final breaths.

🌬️

Crowfeather Living

Senior Warrior of WindClan
  • Clan WindClan
  • Pelt Dark grey, almost black; blue eyes
  • Kits Hollyleaf, Jayfeather, Lionblaze (w/ Leafpool); Breezepelt (w/ Nightcloud)
  • Notable One of the four chosen cats of the new prophecy

A brooding but loyal WindClan warrior who was chosen for the journey to sun-drown-place. He fell in love with ThunderClan medicine cat Leafpool — a relationship forbidden by the warrior code — and their secret kits became central characters in later arcs.

🌊 RiverClan
🐟

Mistystar Living

Leader of RiverClan
  • Mother Bluestar
  • Father Oakheart (RiverClan)
  • Clan RiverClan
  • Pelt Blue-grey, blue eyes
  • Brother Stonefur (deceased)

Daughter of Bluestar, raised in RiverClan. A steady, fair leader who survived many upheavals including the reign of Leopardstar. Mistystar represents continuity and calm wisdom in the RiverClan line of leadership.

🐈

Crookedstar StarClan

Leader of RiverClan
  • Born name Stormkit / Crookedpaw
  • Clan RiverClan
  • Pelt Light brown tabby, crooked jaw
  • Mate Willowbreeze
  • Kit Silverstream

Broke his jaw as a kit and was disfigured for life, yet rose to become a beloved and honorable leader. His origin story — involving a dark bargain with Mapleshade — is told in Crookedstar's Promise. Silverstream's father and a key figure in RiverClan history.

⭐ StarClan, Loners & Notable Others
⭐

Jayfeather Living

ThunderClan Medicine Cat
  • Parents Crowfeather & Leafpool (secret)
  • Clan ThunderClan
  • Pelt Grey tabby, blind blue eyes
  • Siblings Lionblaze, Hollyleaf
  • Power Can walk in other cats' dreams

Born blind, Jayfeather became ThunderClan's medicine cat and one of the Three cats of the Great Prophecy. His ability to enter other cats' dreams makes him uniquely connected to StarClan. He is sharp-tongued, impatient, but deeply gifted.

🦁

Lionblaze Living

ThunderClan Warrior
  • Parents Crowfeather & Leafpool (secret)
  • Clan ThunderClan
  • Pelt Golden tabby, amber eyes
  • Mate Cinderheart
  • Power Cannot be hurt in battle

One of the Three of the Great Prophecy. Lionblaze possesses the power to fight without injury — an ability that made him a fearsome warrior. His arc explores the psychological burden of feeling invincible and the threat to his clan's survival.

🐍

Scourge Deceased

Leader of BloodClan
  • Born name Tiny (kittypet)
  • Clan BloodClan (Twolegplace)
  • Pelt Small, black, ice-blue eyes
  • Collar Studded with teeth and claws
  • Notable Killed Tigerstar; defeated by Firestar

A tiny but terrifying rogue cat who built an army of street cats in Twolegplace. He is notable for killing Tigerstar (all nine lives at once) and leading BloodClan into the forest. His origin as a frightened kittypet named Tiny is explored in The Rise of Scourge manga.
