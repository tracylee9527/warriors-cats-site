Characters

Every cat who shaped the destiny of the Clans.

⚠ Unofficial Fan Site — Not affiliated with Working Partners Ltd. or HarperCollins

The Warriors series spans hundreds of books and features an enormous cast of characters across all Clans, eras, and walks of life. This roster covers named cats from the main arcs, super editions, novellas, and prequel series — organized by Clan affiliation. Status badges reflect each character's fate as of their last appearance.
