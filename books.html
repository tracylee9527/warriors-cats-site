Book Series

Over 100 books. Nine main arcs. One legendary world.

⚠ Unofficial Fan Site — Not affiliated with Working Partners Ltd. or HarperCollins

The Warriors universe is vast, spanning nine main novel series, multiple super editions, novellas, manga, and field guides. The core series follows different cat protagonists across the generations, building a rich and interconnected world. This guide covers each arc, its point-of-view characters, and a brief overview of its themes — without spoiling the stories.

🐾 Where to Start — Recommended Reading Order

  1. The Prophecies Begin (Arc 1) — Start here. This is the original series and introduces the world, Clans, and core characters.
  2. The New Prophecy (Arc 2) — Continues directly after Arc 1 with a new generation of protagonists.
  3. Power of Three (Arc 3) — Follows the grandchildren of key characters from Arc 1.
  4. Omen of the Stars (Arc 4) — Concludes the storyline began in Arc 3. Best read immediately after Arc 3.
  5. Dawn of the Clans (Arc 5) — A prequel series; can be read any time after Arc 1.
  6. A Vision of Shadows (Arc 6) — A new generation story continuing from Arc 4.
  7. The Broken Code (Arc 7) — The most recent main arc. Read after Arc 6.

Main Series Arcs

Arc 1 The Prophecies Begin

Published: 2003–2004  |  6 books

Protagonist: Firestar (Rusty → Firepaw → Fireheart → Firestar)

The original Warriors series that started it all. A young house cat named Rusty is invited to join ThunderClan and must prove himself in a world of ancient traditions, inter-Clan rivalries, and hidden dangers. This arc introduces the warrior code, the four Clans, and the spirit worlds of StarClan and the Dark Forest.

Into the Wild Fire and Ice Forest of Secrets Rising Storm A Dangerous Path The Darkest Hour

Arc 2 The New Prophecy

Published: 2005–2006  |  6 books

Protagonists: Brambleclaw, Feathertail, Stormfur, Tawnypelt, Crowpaw, Squirrelpaw

A new generation of cats receives a prophecy that sends them on a great journey beyond the forest. When they return, the Clans face the loss of their home territory and must make the dangerous journey to a new home by the lake — a journey that will change Clan life forever.

Midnight Moonrise Dawn Starlight Twilight Sunset

Arc 3 Power of Three

Published: 2007–2009  |  6 books

Protagonists: Jayfeather, Lionblaze, Hollyleaf

Firestar's grandchildren — Jayfeather, Lionblaze, and Hollyleaf — discover they each carry special powers. An ancient prophecy says they hold the power of the stars in their paws. As they explore their abilities, dark secrets about their true parentage begin to unravel.

The Sight Dark River Outcast Eclipse Long Shadows Sunrise

Arc 4 Omen of the Stars

Published: 2009–2012  |  6 books

Protagonists: Jayfeather, Lionblaze, Dovewing

The Great Battle between the living Clans and the cats of the Dark Forest approaches. Dovewing, a young ThunderClan warrior with extraordinary senses, discovers her role as the third of the Three. The series builds to a massive confrontation that will determine the fate of all the Clans.

The Fourth Apprentice Fading Echoes Night Whispers Sign of the Moon The Forgotten Warrior The Last Hope

Arc 5 Dawn of the Clans

Published: 2013–2015  |  6 books

Protagonists: Thunder, Gray Wing, Clear Sky

A prequel series set long before the events of the original arc. It follows a group of mountain cats who descend to the forest and eventually form the four Clans. This arc explores the origins of the warrior code, StarClan, and the founding leaders — Thunderstar, Shadowstar, Windstar, and Riverstar.

The Sun Trail Thunder Rising The First Battle The Blazing Star A Forest Divided Path of Stars

Arc 6 A Vision of Shadows

Published: 2016–2018  |  6 books

Protagonist: Alderheart

A new prophecy calls on ThunderClan's young medicine cat Alderheart to find the lost SkyClan — a fifth Clan that was driven from the forest long ago. This arc reintroduces SkyClan as a major player in Clan politics and culminates in all five Clans sharing territory by the lake.

The Apprentice's Quest Thunder and Shadow Shattered Sky Darkest Night River of Fire The Raging Storm

Arc 7 The Broken Code

Published: 2019–2021  |  6 books

Protagonists: Shadowsight, Bristlefrost, Rootspring

A strange darkness threatens to tear the Clans apart from within. An impostor controls a leader's body, StarClan goes silent, and three young cats from different Clans must work together to uncover the truth and save both the living and spirit worlds.

Lost Stars The Silent Thaw Veil of Shadows Darkness Within The Place of No Stars A Light in the Mist

Arc 8 A Starless Clan

Published: 20222024  |  6 books

Protagonists: Frostpaw, Sunbeam, Nightheart

A new generation of cats takes centre stage. The mysterious disappearance of RiverClan's leader sends ripples of instability through all five Clans. Three young warriors from RiverClan, ShadowClan, and ThunderClan must navigate shifting loyalties, dark secrets, and forces beyond StarClan's reach to restore peace to the lake.

River Sky Thunder Shadow Wind Storm

Arc 9 The Veil of Stars

Published: 2024present  |  Ongoing

Current ongoing arc

The newest Warriors arc, currently in progress. Picking up after the events of A Starless Clan, this series continues to explore life by the lake as new threats emerge and the bonds between Clans are tested in ways never seen before. Check warriorcats.com for the latest releases.
